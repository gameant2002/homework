WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW

There is a lot of talk about drugs in the world—on the streets, at school, on the Internet and TV. Some of it is true, some not.Much of what you hear about drugs actually comes from those selling them. Reformed drug dealers have confessed they would have said anything to get others to buy drugs.Don’t be fooled. You need facts to avoid becoming hooked on drugs and to help your friends stay off them.
